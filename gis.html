What is "GIS" ???

GIS Geographic Information System (Geographic Information System) is a computer system used to enter, store, check, integrate, manipulate, analyze, and display data related to positions on the earth's surface. GIS is identical to the use of computers because computers have many advantages, especially speed and efficiency. GIS is used as a tool or media used for mapping and analysis of various activities on the earth's surface.
